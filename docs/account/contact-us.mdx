# Contact us

Argos is open-source and community-driven. We are supported by a network of early advocates, contributors, and champions.

## Join the community

The best way to contact us is to join the Argos [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/M8ATmvqv).

Drop by to meet other users, ask questions, and share your work. We will listen to our users' feature requests and compose with Argos' roadmap.

## Contact e-mail

If you are not a Discord user, you can reach us by sending an e-mail to: contact@argos-ci.com.
